ABOUT

Hey, I'm Lillie! Web Developer & Designer

I'm originally from a tiny town in New Zealand called Whakatu. I moved to Naarm in 2014 and haven't looked back. I live here with my partner (Samuel), our son (Harlow), and our two fluffy sausage dogs (Puku & Pirate).

I'm an active relaxer; I relax by doing stuff and keeping my hands busy. I love to knit, draw, cook, lift heavy weights at the gym, read fantasy books, and landscape our backyard. Although, I don't have a green thumb so it's a guessing game which plants decide to stick around.

Most of my time goes into my knitting business, Chunky Boy. I hand-knit one-off and limited-edition clothing pieces that ship all over the world. It started as a hobby to unwind but organically turned into a business in 2017. Through a lot of google searches, trial and error, and countless YouTube videos. I started teaching myself coding basics. Turns out I really enjoy the tech side of things as much as the creative side.

Working on my own website, everything just clicked. My brain liked what I was learning. I joined Coder Academy to dig deeper, build on what I've taught myself, learn to code and open new doors for myself in the field of tech.
